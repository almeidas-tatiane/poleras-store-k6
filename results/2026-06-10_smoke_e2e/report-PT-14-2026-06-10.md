## E2E p95 note
The e2e global P95 of 526ms (vs 194ms on 2026-06-09) is caused by the unpatched payment gateway introducing 200–800ms latency into the full purchase journey. Auth, products, cart and orders alone contribute ~58ms at most. The p50 of 13.25ms confirms that the majority of requests are fast; only the payment-heavy tail drives up the aggregate P95.
